Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11/12/2025

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

The app operates entirely in your browser on your device. It does not request, store, or transmit any personal information.

The app does not make network requests for analytics, tracking, or advertising. It may load static assets, such as SVG files. These requests do not involve tracking, cookies, or personal data collection.

The application contains Amazon affiliate links. When you click these links and make purchases, Amazon may use cookies or other tracking according to their privacy policy. No tracking occurs within the DartsXL Counter application itself.

Local Storage

The app may use your browser's local storage to save game state, match history, or settings. All data remains on your device and is not transmitted externally.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs.

Changes to This Policy

If the app's behavior changes in a future update, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly.
